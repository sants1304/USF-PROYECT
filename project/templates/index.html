General facts

The energy sector is very important for jobs and innovation in societies.

It is also the largest contributor to carbon emissions. In places that do not have a source of energy they tend to use diesel generators which are harmful to the environment. 1

  • Covid-19 policies that were implemented around the world have exasperated the energy crisis.
  • From 2010 to 2019 people without access to electricity went from 1.2 billion to 759 million. 2
  • Working toward sustainability the use of mini grids has increased from 5 million to 11 million from 2010 to 2019. 2

Why should you care?

According to the Millenium Alliance for Humanity and the Biosphere the worlds oil reserves will only last until about 2052 at the current rate of consumption. Furthermore, other fossil fuels such as natural gas will only last until 2060, and all coal will be used up by 2090. 3

  • Coal we are currently using came from dead plants about 300 million years ago, and oil and natural gas are about 200 million years old. This means that there is no chance for us to be able to renew these resources, and we need to find another means quickly if we want to keep having electricity.
  • Today 60.8% of all power in the United States comes from fossil fuels, 75% in Latin America, and 37% in Europe. It is possible to cut down on our usage, but it will be much more difficult to switch over if we are already out of power. 4
  • As of right now there are 759 million people without electricity access and 2.6 billion without access to clean cooking equipment. Clean energy could allow them these things that we consider necessities and take for granted. 5

Future Development

Having in mind the humans' necessity of surviving by the natural resources in a sustainable way, knowing about the concern of affordable and clean energy will help citizens to improve solutions or options to diverse problems as:

Energy consumption

An raised individual, social and cultural awareness about taking care of nature and people by helping in solving their needs. In this case, awareness will avoid wrong energy consumptions at homes.

Health technology

Using technology for protecting humans or animals' lives. For example, by an adequate management of electricity services, hospitals and veterinarians will be powered to assist in the best way.

Sustainable economy

Bringing options to citizens for increasing a sustainable economic recovery by different habits and processes. A helpful example is the wise usage of material for making some improved solar light bulbs or solar ovens, in order to change and solve solutions sustainably in the world.
